History Uses Aloe Vera Products

Aloe vera, also known as aloe barbendis is a plant similar to a cactus that thrives in hot, dry weather. Native to southern border regions of the United States and the Middle East, this plant has been used in a variety of applications for millenia. Ancient inhabitants of places like Mexico, China, Egypt, and the Middle East revered the plant for it's versatility. Aloe vera was first formally indentified in the western world by Carl Linnaeus in 1753.
